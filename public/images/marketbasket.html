What you'll do

9:00AM / 14:00 PM: I’m or Co-Host will pick up at Meeting Point/your hotel

9:20AM/14:20PM: Our guide will take you to Ba Le local Market. Our Guide will introduce to you the necessary ingredient as well as the daily life of local people

9:50am/14:50pm: Hop on the unique bamboo basket boats and start your journey exploring the small channels , go into the forest of water coconut/nipa , learn about the history of the water coconut forest during Vietnam wars , visit around coconut island.

Tourist will be guided to rowing boat, fishing, catch the river snails, and fishing with the local fisherman, experience a mazing performance with basket boat dancing and visiting the local houses made by coconut leaves along river.

10:30Am/15:30Pm: You will be welcomed with welcome drink fresh juice and participate in cooking class with our Chef.

Learn the traditional ways of preparing and cooking authentic Vietnamese food. After the lesson, enjoy the deliciouis dishes that you prepared

12:30/17:30 : We will take you back the your hotel or place upon you request

Note: Vegetarian option is available, we will change ingredients for you

MENU FOR YOUR COOK:

1/Hoi An spring rolls (pork and shrimp)
2/Hoi An Pancake (Banh xeo) with pork and prawn
3/Sauteed fish in clay pot or chicken curry or Pho Noole(request)
4/Sauteed morning glory with garlic
